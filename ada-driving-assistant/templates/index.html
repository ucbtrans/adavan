❓ Help

🚧 Route Conditions
  • "What hazards are ahead on my route?"
  • "Are there any construction zones on my route?"
  • "What are the road conditions along my route?"
🏙️ Street Conditions
  • "What's happening on Telegraph Avenue?"
  • "Any incidents on Shattuck Ave?"
  • "Is University Avenue clear?"
🅿️ Parking — uncolored curbs
  • "Is there parking near my destination?"
  • "Any parking within two blocks from Solano Ave and Tacoma Ave?"
  • "What's the parking situation near Telegraph and Bancroft?"
♿ Blue Curb (disabled parking)
  • "Is there blue curb parking near my destination?"
  • "Any disabled parking near Shattuck and University?"
  • "Where can I find a handicap spot near Telegraph and Dwight?"
📍 Map Markers
  • "Show markers" — display obstacle markers on the map
  • "Clear markers" — remove all obstacle markers from the map
💬 Step-by-step Movement Commands
  • "Advance 2 blocks" / "Move forward 3" — move forward along the route
  • "Go back 2 blocks" / "Back 1" — reverse along the route
  • Accepts numbers or words: "three blocks", "3 blocks", "3"
▶ Continuous Route Simulation
  • ▶ / ⏸ — start or pause forward driving
  • ◀ — drive in reverse
  • ⏩ / ⏪ — fast-forward or fast-rewind at 10× real time
  • ⏹ — stop and reset to route origin
  • Speed shown reflects actual simulated speed including deceleration
  • Simulated travel time is displayed next to the controls
🚦 Traffic Lights (simulation)
  • Traffic light icons are shown at intersections along the route
  • Each light cycles independently through green → yellow → red
  • Car automatically slows and stops at red/yellow, resumes on green
  • Traffic lights are ignored on freeways
🛑 Stop Signs (simulation)
  • Stop sign icons are shown at intersections along the route
  • Car automatically slows and stops briefly at each stop sign
  • Stop signs are ignored on freeways
🛣️ Freeway Driving
  • Speed is 65 mph on freeways — no stops for traffic lights or stop signs
  • Freeway segments are detected automatically from the route
🎤 Voice
  • Say "Hey ADA" to activate voice input
  • Use the 🎤 mic button to speak any question
💡 Tips
  • Type help in the chat at any time to see this guide
  • Parking estimates are simulated and apply to unregulated (non-colored) curbs only
  • Blue curb spots require a disabled placard or qualifying license plate
  • Coverage: Berkeley, Albany, El Cerrito, Richmond, Emeryville, and Oakland, CA
